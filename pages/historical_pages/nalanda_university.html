About

Nalanda University Nalanda University, renowned as the world’s famous university is located at Rajgir, Bihar. The word Nalanda is derived from the Sanskrit word “Nalan” which mean “lotus” which is a symbol of knowledge and wisdom.Hence, Nalanda meaning knowledge giver. It was situated in erstwhile Magadh today’s Bihar. This place is connected with Buddha and hence holds immense value to the Buddhist community. Nalanda was Asia’s biggest and most prestigious university that had student from countries like Japan, China, Korea, Turkey, Tibet, Mongol, Persia, etc. Nalanda being the world’s center for knowledge had respect of many kings and kingdoms. But getting into Nalanda was not an easy job. One had to pass an entrance exam in order to get admission. It is famous for its detailed description of excellent education system and purity of monastic life practiced there. Nearby tourist attractions are Nalanda museum and Nava Nalanda Mahavir.
