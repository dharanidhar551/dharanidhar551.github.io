About

Lake Palace Lake Palace is located on the island of Jag Niwas in Lake Pichoala, Udaipur, India. Taj Lake Palace is one of the 4 palaces of the Royal Dynasty of Mewar. Over two-an-a-half centuries ago, Maharana Jagat Singhji decided to build his own pleasure palace on an island in the middle of Lake Pichola. Lake Palace is one of the most romantic places on the earth. The Palace situated amidst the scenic Pichola Lake offers a heavenly view to the onlooker. The imposing Aravalli Hills on one side and soaring palaces on the other, make the perfect background to this beautiful Lake Palace. Originally, the Palace was named as Jag Niwas after the name of the Maharana. In the present times, it is popular by the name of Lake Palace. The wonderful architecture of the Lake Palace will send shivers down your spine. Lake Palace has a series of courtyards that are aligned with numerous columns, terraces, beautiful fountains and well laid gardens. The nearest places to visit the Lake Palace are Ambrai Ghat, city palace of Udaipur, Jagdish Temple and Jagmandir. These are the moreover places which will take about 10-20 minutes to reach the Lake Palace. Guests take a boat-ride provided by the Hotel Staff to reach the Lake Palace from the banks of Lake Pichola. By Air: The Lake Palace is located 26 kms from the Udaipur Airport. By Train: It is located 3 kms from the Udaipur Railway station.
