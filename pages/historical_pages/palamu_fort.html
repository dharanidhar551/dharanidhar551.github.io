Palamu Fort The Palamu Fort or Palamu kila are two ruined forts located around 20 kilometers southeast of the city of Daltoganj in Jharkhand. The old fort in the plains, which existed even before the Chero dynasty, was built by the king of Raksel dynasty. Those are two large forts located deep in the forests of Palamu on Sher Shah Suri path near Daltoganj. However, it was during the reign of King Medini Ray, who ruled for 13 years from 1658 to 1674 in Palamu. The old fort was rebuilt into a defensive structure. Ray was a Chero tribal king. His rule extended in areas in south gaya and Hazaribagh. He attacked Doisa how known as Navratnagarh and defeated the Nagvanshi Raja Ragunath Shah. With war bounty he constructed the lower fort close to satbarwa, and this fort became famous in the history. Best places to visit near Palamu fort: 1. Betla National park: One of the oldest wildlife sanctuaries of India. Betla National park is famous for wildlife spotting. The National park on has a beautiful landscape with hills, valleys and streams on all sides. 2. Lodh waterfalls: Far inside the deep forest of Palamu lies the beautiful Lodh waterfalls. This waterfall is a massive 468 feet high and has earned the status of being the highest waterfall in Jharkhand. 3. Bhim Chulha: Bhim Chulha is the same stove on which Bhima used to cook food during his years of confinement. It is more than 5000 years old and is located near the banks of Koyla River. 4. Shahpur village: It is situated opposite to Daltonganj, Shahpur village is well known for its white temple and another prominent Masonry building. Transportation: By Air: The nearest airport is Ranchi 165 km away from Medininagar. From there you can hire a bus. By Train: Daltonganj (17.6km away) is the nearest railway station. Buses and outstation cabs are available from Daltonganj for the onward journey. By Road: One can reach Palamu from the nearby big cities of west Bengal, Bihar and Jharkhand by buses as well as cabs. When your heart calls out for a leisure trip and you start searching for a truly relaxing destination, Palamu is the best place to opt for.
